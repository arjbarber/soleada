# soleada

Soleada  is a platform that connects children, teens, adults, and small businesses, respectively, across communities and cultures in order to offer a supportive environment for innovation, collaboration, and creative problem-solving 1. 
Nika’s direct approach to this is using, possibly anonymized, user given profile attributes and matching them to the most similar users by their profile attributes, respectively . It does this in three branches:
LifeStory Pools
Seeking relation and advice, users provide stories about their own lives: hardships they went through, problems they’re having, etc.
This story is anonymized, doxxable details removed from, embedded, and sent to a pool of similar stories, where others can see, comment, and interact with the user's post. 
This pool of similar stories is semi-public, by being only available to those who have posted similar stories, thus having gone through similar experiences and possibly able to find relatability or give advice to the OP.
Maybe use gemini for verifying that a post is Child Friendly
Story Pools
Similar to lifestory pools but more child friendly and equipped to learn languages better, as well as allow for more ai-assisted creativity.
This story can be anonymized, doxxable details removed from, embedded, and sent to a pool of similar stories, where others can see, comment, and interact with the user's post. 
This pool of similar stories is semi-public, by being only available to those who have posted similar or top stories.
Founder Match
Seeking other founders or investors, users provide an elevator pitch about their own product.
This pitch is anonymized, doxxable details removed from, embedded, and sent to a pool of similar products.
Pitch Matching:
The OPs of the posts that are found to be similar are matched and a chat is created in order for the two to further discuss their ideas.
Investors with their own criteria are automatically given posts that match it.
Corporate Conversations
Seeking businesses or organizations to work 
with, users provide details about the values and goals of their business.
These details are anonymized, doxxable details removed from, embedded, and sent to a pool of similar values and goals.
Profile Matching
Matched organizations and businesses are given chat windows to further discuss how they could work together.
Possibly users who are looking for businesses to shop at are matched with those with their own respected values and goals.
